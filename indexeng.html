Acorn

We have been creating an app for beginners to learn how to play guitar. At the moment there is couple of quizes, one practise for playing guitar, and also we have prototype of guitar tuner, but it is not publishd yet. The app is created by using Angular and Node modules. For databases we have used MySQL. In the app there is in-game money what you can get by doing some quizes or practises. With that money you can buy cool hats, guitars or books on our store.
You can check our website and demo on acorn.ticorporate.fi
